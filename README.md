## Prism Insurance Company Analysis

# Prism Insurance Pvt. Ltd. – Power BI Insurance Analytics Dashboard

##  Project Overview

This project presents an end-to-end Insurance Analytics Dashboard built using Power BI to analyze policy performance, premium collections,
claim trends, customer demographics, and policy status across an insurance company.

---

#  Business Problem Statement

### Policy Performance

* Which policy type generates the highest premium revenue?
* Which insurance products contribute most to company earnings?
* How many policies are currently active versus inactive?

### Claims Analysis

* Which policy categories generate the highest claim amounts?
* What is the status distribution of claims (Rejected, Settled, Pending)?
* Which age groups file the largest claims?

### Customer Analysis

* What is the gender distribution of customers?
* Are there demographic patterns affecting claim behavior?

### Business Monitoring

* What is the total premium amount collected?
* What is the total coverage amount provided?
* What is the overall claim amount paid or requested?

#  Key Findings & Insights

## 1. Premium Revenue Analysis

### Finding:

Travel Insurance generated the highest premium revenue.

| Policy Type | Premium Amount |
| ----------- | -------------- |
| Travel      | 2.5M           |
| Health      | 1.2M           |
| Auto        | 1.0M           |
| Life        | 0.7M           |
| Home        | 0.6M           |

### Insight:

* Travel insurance contributes approximately 42% of total premium revenue.
* Home and Life insurance contribute significantly less and may require product optimization or stronger marketing strategies.

---

## 2. Policy Activity Analysis

### Finding:

* Active Policies: 5.81K (58.11%)
* Inactive Policies: 4.19K (41.89%)

### Insight:

* More than half of all policies remain active, indicating healthy customer retention.
* However, the inactive policy percentage is relatively high and presents opportunities for renewal campaigns and customer engagement programs.

---

## 3. Claim Status Analysis

### Finding:

Claim statuses are distributed across:

* Rejected Claims: 4.4K
* Settled Claims: 3.4K
* Pending Claims: 2.3K

### Insight:

* Rejected claims represent the largest category.
* A high rejection rate may indicate stricter claim validation processes or documentation issues.
* Pending claims should be monitored to improve claim settlement turnaround times.

---

## 4. Age Group Claim Analysis

### Finding:

| Age Group   | Claim Amount |
| ----------- | ------------ |
| Adult       | 8.8M         |
| Elder       | 6.4M         |
| Young Adult | 1.7M         |

### Insight:

* Adults account for the highest claim amount.
* Young adults contribute the least to total claims.
* Insurance products targeting adults should incorporate stronger risk assessment and pricing strategies.
